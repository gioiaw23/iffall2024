The Universal Chia Seed Pudding Recipe

A creamy, delicious, and healthy breakfast or snack option!

Ingredients (One Serving)

  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond or coconut milk
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • fruits of your choice (recommendation: berries, bananas, mangos, apples, peaches etc.)
  • crunchies of your choice (granola, nuts)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together the chia seeds, almond milk, honey or maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  2. Stir well to combine and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir again to prevent clumping, then cover the bowl or jar.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight, until thickened.
  5. Serve chilled with your favorite toppings such as fruits or nuts.
